Hello 👋

I am Sang Choe, a third-year CS PhD student in Language Technologies Institute at Carnegie Mellon University, advised by Eric Xing.

My vision is to democratize AI for everyone. Just like traditional software technologies, I believe AI adoption can be significantly accelerated when both developer & user experiences for AI are improved and thereby a positive feedback loop evolves around these two parties. More specifically, to boost AI developer experience, I work on standardizing and automating the AI development pipeline. To enhance AI user experience, I research on language user interface (LUI) with which users instruct the computer to perform tasks via natural language. Some of the technical keywords for my reserach are: machine learning systems, meta-learning & AutoML, reinforcement learning, and natural language processing.

Previously, I completed MS in Language Technologies at Carnegie Mellon University under the guidance of Jaime Carbonell. Before that, I earned BS in Electrical Computer Engineering & Mathematics (double major) from Seoul National University. I had also spent time as a research intern at Microsoft in 2021.
